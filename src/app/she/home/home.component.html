SHE

SHE is a website which soley focuses on women empowerment, like providing working hostel facilities, providing courses for the women who want to develop their skills so that they live independently and providing a small deposit scheme for the girl child who is below 10 years.

STEP

The Support to Training and Employment Programme for Women (STEP) Scheme aims to provide skills that give employability to women and to provide competencies and skill that enable women to become self-employed/ entrepreneurs.

NGO

Non-governmental organizations, or NGOs are generally nonprofit entities independent of governmental influence, they play a critical part in developing society, improving communities, and promoting citizen participation.
